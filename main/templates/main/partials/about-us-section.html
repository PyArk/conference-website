Python Artists of Arkansas

Welcome!

We're a group of Python enthusiasts in the Little Rock, Arkansas area exploring all types of Python programming. Python developers of all levels are encouraged to join us for our monthly meetups and help out with our community projects.

Whether just curious, a new programmer, or you remember clearing the moths out of your vacuum tubes, we want to see YOU! Primarily about the Python programming language, we want to encourage all "programming" and "technology" discussions - COME GEEK OUT!

We are the official Python.org sponsored user group for central Arkansas. Our website is http://pyark.org/
